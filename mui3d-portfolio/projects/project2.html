Fantasy Weapon Set

A collection of stylized fantasy weapons designed for a game-ready pipeline. Each piece was sculpted with attention to silhouette and material storytelling — from worn leather grips to enchanted crystal inlays. The set includes a greatsword, battle axe, and dagger, all optimized for real-time engines with clean topology and PBR textures.
