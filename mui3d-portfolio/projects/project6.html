Space Station Flythrough

A cinematic camera flythrough of a modular space station orbiting a distant nebula. The station was built from reusable kitbash modules with emissive window panels and animated docking arms. Camera motion was hand-keyed along a spline path with depth-of-field and volumetric lighting to sell the vastness of deep space.
