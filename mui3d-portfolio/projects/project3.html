Mech Walker Animation

A fully rigged and animated bipedal mech designed for a cinematic walk cycle. The project focused on weight and mechanical believability — piston-driven leg movement, secondary motion on antenna arrays, and dust-kick particle effects synced to footfalls. The final animation was rendered with dramatic rim lighting to emphasize the hard-surface details.
