My work spans precision engineering, symbolic sculptures, creative concepts, DIY builds, and procedural design systems. Through Mui3D, I document the process of designing, prototyping, and bringing ideas into reality.

I also share tutorials, workflows, and behind-the-scenes insights to help others explore 3D design, fabrication, and creative problem solving.
